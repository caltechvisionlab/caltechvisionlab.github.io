                       Pasadena
This is a demo in which you can explore our algorithm's performance on the Pasadena dataset as compared with the ground truth inventory. Here you can see where the program has misidentified a tree or missed one altogether, among other features. However, many of our inconsistencies with the ground truth, (e.g. where the ground truth says our algorithm has identified a tree where a tree does not exist) are, in fact, errors with the ground truth due to GPS localization error or out of date inventory rather than errors with our algorithm.
