﻿Outreach Partnerships with SWIRLSS

The inspiration for this project has always been based in stewardship of our local environment. Our algorithm gives us the tools to better understand, protect, and grow our local street trees. It provides opportunities for stewardship and education too good to pass up, so we've partnered with SWIRLSS to integrate our project and its motivations into local secondary school curriculum. 
Contact SWIRLSS

CITY CHARTER

At City Charter, 9th grade biology students will engage in a year-long curriculum grounded in the value of trees and their impact on our local environment and society. They will learn how to explore their questions and concerns with research, framed by our project's process and applications. 

PARTNERSHIP FOR SUCCESS

In the Partnership for Success (PFS) summer program, 4th and 5th grade girls from across the Pasadena Unified School District will engage in a brief introduction to the role of trees in our lives and how research like ours can better help us manage our important local trees.

Partnership with TreePeople

﻿Throughout the project we have worked with TreePeople, a community organization devoted to preserving and growing Los Angeles' urban forest. The group has helped us not only to explore applications for the algorithm but also in our deep learning process, providing the crucial expertise in tree species necessary for training our algorithm. 

TreePeople and its members are excited about our algorithm's potential to support their efforts of monitoring progress in city reforestation, analyzing tree health, planning planting events, and educating the public about the value of trees.
