Dataset

Request Download
The Pasadena Urban Trees dataset includes roughly 30,000 trees labeled by geo-location and tree species located within Pasadena.   The dataset includes dense aerial and street view imagery for this region, as well as the train/test splits used in the paper.   Due to licensing restrictions, it may only be used for research purposes and is available by sending a request to registree@googlegroups.com.
